About Our Charity

Munagi is a non profit organization focused on identifying individual needs and community resources which are used to build self sufficiency. Munagi is an Igbo word that means "Me and You." We recognize the world is interconnected and we are interdependent on each other. We also recognize economic inequalities perpetuate educational iniquity, which creates a vicious cycle of generational poverty. Please join us in this journey as we work towards creating opportunities for a better life, one child, one village, at a time.

Munagi raises money for scholarships for young women to receive an education who otherwise would not be able to attend school at St. Cathrine's in Nkwerre, IMO State Nigeria

Our Mission

Munagi's purpose is to foster children's giftedness by providing them with the appropriate resources and opportunity. Through enrichment activities, individualized support, community engagement, parental involvement, scholarships, advocacy, and awareness , they hope to equip children to become change agents in their communities. Munagi's goal is to nurture the development and good effect of brilliant children by creating a friendly atmosphere.

Our Vision

"At Munagi's Foundation, we envision a world in which every child's exceptionality is recognized, nurtured, and celebrated." We aim to empower children by giving them the resources, opportunity, and support they need to reach their greatest potential. Our mission is to raise a generation of change agents who will use their skills to make a difference in their communities and beyond. Through our commitment to promoting giftedness, we hope to create a culture that recognizes and loves each child's unique gifts and contributions. We can work together to build a future in which gifted children thrive, paving the path for a brighter and more inclusive world."
